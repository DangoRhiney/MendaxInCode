Character Synopsis

Sun Wukong, also know as the Monkey King is a mythological figure who features in a body of legends, which can be traced back to the period of the Song Dynasty. He appears as a main character in the 16th century classical Chinese novel Journey To The West. Sun Wukong is also found in many later stories and adaptations. In the novel, he is a monkey born from a stone who acquires supernatural powers through Taoist practices. After rebelling against heaven and being imprisoned under a mountain by the Buddha, he later accompanies the monk Xuanzang on a journey to retrieve Buddhist sutras from "the West".
